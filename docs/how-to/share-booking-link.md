# How to Share a Booking Link

Invito provides two types of booking links. Choose the one that fits your use case.

## Link types

### Generic link (guest chooses the event type)

```
https://invito.example.com/calendar/{username}/
```

The guest lands on a page that lists all your active event types and picks one. Use this when you want to give someone access to all your meeting types — for example, in an email signature or on your website.

### Pre-filled link (event type is fixed)

```
https://invito.example.com/calendar/{username}/{slug}
```

The guest goes directly to the slot picker for a specific event type. Use this when you know what kind of meeting you want — for example, "book a 30-min intro call with me" in a proposal or outreach email.

## Finding your links

1. Go to **Dashboard → Event Types**.
2. Each event type shows its direct booking URL with a **Copy link** button.
3. Your generic link is shown at the top of the page.

## Sharing tips

- **Email:** Paste the link as plain text or as a hyperlink ("Pick a time →").
- **Website:** Add a "Book a call" button linking to your generic or specific booking URL.
- **Calendar invite placeholder:** Send a calendar invite with your booking link in the description so the other party can pick a time to replace the placeholder.

## What the guest experiences

1. The guest opens the link.
2. They see a date navigation and click through to a day with available slots.
3. They pick a time slot, fill in their name and email, and optionally add a note.
4. They see a confirmation page: "Your request has been sent."
5. You receive a notification email. You confirm or reject the booking.
6. The guest receives a confirmation (or rejection) email.

Guests do not need an Invito account.
